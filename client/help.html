candidates faq

How much does it cost to use Ceek?

We help so!ware engineers and designers find great companies to work at. Candidates don't pay us anything, we're paid by the companies.

How does the Ceek process work?

One click sign up - To save your time, you sign up with your LinkedIn credentials and upload the PDF export of your LinkedIn profile.

Create Profile – We use your PDF export of your LinkedIn profile to build a basic profile for you. You answer a few simple questions. We generate an analysis of your work style and a recommendation for the ideal job role.

Intelligent Matching - Our matching algorithm finds the best possible matches among the top tech jobs on the market. As soon as you create your profile, we will start matching and pushing you to the matching companies immediately. Companies can easily request an interview with a single click. A timer will ensure company act quickly.

Bidding on you – The companies can bid on you by making a non-binding offer that is higher than the compensation listed on their job posting or the market rate. The bidding offer will be highlighted to gain your attention.

Review the opportunities - You'll receive interview requests from companies - with salary, equity, and role information upfront. You'll then be able to decide which companies to interview with and start the interview process. Get Hired - We introduce you and the company with a mutual email. Companies contact you to set up the onsite interview. You choose the one that is the best fit and get hired.

What types of companies do you work with?

We work with venture-backed startups and also larger technology companies. We don't represent specific companies though, rather we see ourselves as working to help good engineers to find companies they'll love working at.

Will you contact my current or past employers?

No. We won’t. We will identify all your current and past employers from your LinkedIn profile so that they will not be contacted. All candidates have the option to identify specific companies that Ceek shouldn't contact on their behalf.

If I accept an interview request, will Ceek continue to find companies for me?

Yes! Once you activate your profile, we will match and push you to the companies regardless of how many interview requests you accept. After 2 weeks, your profile will become Inactive. You can activate it again until you find your dream job.

I'm just interested in seeing what I'm worth, is Ceek the platform for me?

Ceek relies on high quality talent that is serious about finding new opportunities. If that's not you, check in with us again when you are ready.

I just found my dream job, can you remove me from Ceek?

There is an “on or off the market” setting under your profile. As soon as you switch “on” to “off”, we will stop the job search immediately. If you turn it “on”, Ceek will automatically start the match making and finding you your dream job again.

companies faq

Why I am receiving an email from Ceek informing me that they have found the top candidate for my company?

Ceek proactively identifies the companies looking for great hires. Ceek matches and delivers the top candidates to you if your company is looking for engineers and developers to hire.

Why should I use Ceek?

To grow your business you need to hire top candidate. Ceek delivers active and responsive candidates to you continuously until you find the right one, so you can spend less time on sourcing & screening and more time on interviewing & hiring.

How much does it cost to use Ceek?

Recruiting agency typically charge 20% - 30% of the hire’s first year salary. For a limited time, Ceek is free to companies. Candidates don't pay us anything.

How does the Ceek process work?

Candidate Profile Analysis – As soon as candidate creates the profile, we generate an analysis of their work style and a recommendation for the ideal job role. This info will be available to you for your review.

Intelligent Matching – Our matching algorithm finds the best possible matches for your company. You'll get an email once we have best matches for your company. You can easily accept or pass on a match with a single click. We will ensure that you always have best matches in the queue before the position is successfully filled.

Hiring successfully – The candidate accept your interview request with a single click. A timer will ensure that they respond quickly. We introduce you and the candidates with a mutual email. You contact the candidates to set up the onsite interview and hire the best candidate for your company.

Any advice for recruiting successfully?

Once you’re engaged with a candidate, you need to move quickly. The longer you wait, the more companies and offers you’re going to be competing against.
